Welcome to PHP Sweden

PHP Sweden is a non-profit organization that want to bring togheter the Swedish PHP community. We are working to increase the knowledge and usage of PHP and to help and teach each other. PHP Sweden started in 2012 under the name Symfony Sweden but the name was changed 2016 because we wanted to include the wider PHP-community.

Since we started, we've hosted over 50 meetups in Stockhom and Gothenburg. We have also organized two conferences; November camp 2013 and November Camp 2014. At the time of writing we have over 900 members in our meetup groups. If you are interested in joining us on our meetups you should check out or pages at meetup.com: PHP GÃ¶teborg and PHP Stockholm.

Join us

If you agree with our mission and would like to join us, you are very much welcome to.
Beeing a member includes the following perks:

  • You will be invited to our secret Slack channel
  • You will be subscribed to our mailing list (we send maximum 5 emails per year)
  • You will be invited to participate on our annual meeting

Applying to a membership is easy. Just send a PR to our membership repository with some details about yourself. On our latest annual meeting we decided that all memberships should be free.
